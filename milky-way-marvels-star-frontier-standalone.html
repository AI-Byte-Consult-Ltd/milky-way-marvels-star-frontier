🛰️ How to Play

  • The chart in the center shows the known galaxy. Click any star to set course and fly there.
  • Fuel is spent on every flight, based on distance. It regenerates slowly on its own, or you can buy it instantly with Star Credits from the sidebar or the Market.
  • Arriving at a new star automatically surveys its planets, granting a one-time haul of that planet's material (Metal, Ice, Crystal, or Organics).
  • Any surveyed planet can be colonized using Credits + Metal. Colonies produce a steady trickle of materials and Credits over time — your main source of long-term income.
  • Colonies can be raided by rival factions. Undefended colonies are more likely to fall — invest in their Defense Grid to protect them. Even unclaimed surveyed planets can be snapped up by rivals if you wait too long.
  • Lost or rival-held planets show as Contested — you can reclaim them, at a premium.
  • Sell surplus materials at the Market for Credits, and reinvest in fuel, colonies, and defenses.
  • Connect Wallet (top right) lets holders of the real Milky Way Marvels NFT collection or RaVerse token on Cronos claim a one-time Founder's Credit bonus. This only reads your public on-chain balances — no transaction or signature is ever requested.
  • Your progress saves automatically in this browser.
Milky Way Marvels: Star Frontier is a companion game for the Milky Way Marvels NFT collection and the RaVerse token on Cronos. Connecting a wallet only checks public on-chain balances (read-only) to grant a cosmetic bonus — it never requests a transaction, a signature, or your private key, and no in-game currency here has real-world value.
